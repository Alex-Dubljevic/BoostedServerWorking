Pokémon Scarlet is the first open-world RPGs in the Pokémon series. As you enjoy the hallmarks of the series—
meeting, teaming up with, battling, training, and trading Pokémon—you'll be able to immerse yourself in a newly 
evolved adventure. Explore a wide-open world at your own pace, battle and try to catch wild Pokémon, and 
encounter the Legendary Pokémon Koraidon.
