Elden Ring developed by FromSoftware, Inc. and BANDAI NAMCO Entertainment Inc., is a fantasy action-RPG 
adventure set within a world created by Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R.R. Martin. Danger and discovery lurk 
around every corner in FromSoftware's largest game to-date. Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish 
the power of the Elden Ring and become an Elden Lord in the Lands Between.
